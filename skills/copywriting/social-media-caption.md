## What it does

Generates 5 engaging, platform-optimized social media captions for products, services, or announcements. Each caption includes relevant hashtags and can be customized by tone and platform.

---

## How to use it

1. Copy the system prompt below
2. Paste it into Claude
3. Describe your product or announcement
4. Get 5 captions ready to post
5. Choose your favorite or remix them

### Time needed: 1-2 minutes

---

## System Prompt

```
You are a social media copywriting expert. Your job is to create engaging, platform-specific captions that drive engagement and conversions.

When given a product, service, or announcement, you will:
1. Generate 5 different captions
2. Each caption should be unique in tone and angle
3. Include 3-5 relevant hashtags per caption
4. Make them compelling, clear, and action-oriented
5. Tailor each one for maximum engagement on that specific platform

Format your response as:

**Caption 1: [Tone]**
[Caption text with emojis and hashtags]

**Caption 2: [Tone]**
[Caption text with emojis and hashtags]

[Continue for all 5]

Variations:
- Use emotional triggers (curiosity, urgency, excitement)
- Include social proof when relevant (reviews, testimonials)
- Add CTAs (calls-to-action) like "DM," "Comment," "Link in bio"
- Use platform-appropriate emojis
- Keep them concise (fit on one screen)

Remember: Great captions hook attention in the first line and make people want to engage.
```

```

Target audience: Remote workers, programmers, designers, office workers.
```

## Tips & Variations

### Customize for different platforms:

**Instagram:** Use more emojis, lifestyle angles, hashtags (#)
**LinkedIn:** More professional, benefit-focused, industry language
**Twitter:** Short, punchy, conversational, trending topics
**TikTok:** Trendy, casual, personality-driven, challenges
**Facebook:** Community-focused, testimonials, longer descriptions

### Make it yours:
- **Change the tone** — Funny, serious, urgent, inspirational
- **Add a discount code** — "Use code LAUNCH20 for 20% off"
- **Include CTAs** — "Comment your answer," "DM for early access"
- **Adjust for platform** — Specific character limits, culture
- **Test variations** — Try different angles and see what performs

### Pro tips:
- Post the same product with 5 different angles to see what resonates
- Use the best-performing caption as your template for future posts
- Update CTAs based on what you want (traffic, sales, engagement, awareness)
- Include the product link or "link in bio"
- Test posting times for your audience

## Common Issues

**Q: The captions feel too generic**
A: Add more specific details about your product in the input (unique features, customer results, your story)

**Q: Not enough variation between captions**
A: Specify different angles (curiosity, urgency, social proof, lifestyle, discount)

**Q: Captions are too long**
A: Ask Claude to shorten them or focus on just 2-3 key platforms

**Q: Hashtags don't fit my niche**
A: Add to the prompt: "Focus hashtags on [your specific industry/audience]"
